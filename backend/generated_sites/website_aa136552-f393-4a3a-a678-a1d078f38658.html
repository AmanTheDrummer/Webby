The Grand Tour's Next Adventure?

By Jeremy Clarkson July 25, 2024

Rumours are swirling about what the trio of Clarkson, Hammond, and May might be up to next after their highly successful Grand Tour series. Future of Supercars: Electric vs. Hyper-Hybrid

By Richard Hammond July 20, 2024

The supercar landscape is undergoing a dramatic transformation. With regulations tightening and technology advancing rapidly, manufacturers are facing a crucial choice: go full electric, or perfect the hyper-hybrid powertrain? We take a look at the pros and cons of each approach, from instant torque to the intoxicating roar of a V12.

Electric supercars like the Rimac Nevera are setting new benchmarks for acceleration, proving that silent power can be absolutely terrifyingly fast. However, the emotional connection to a gasoline engine, the symphony of combustion, is something many purists are not ready to let go of. Hybrid systems offer a bridge, combining electric boost with traditional engines for unparalleled performance and efficiency. Which path will dominate the future? Only time will tell, but it's going to be an exciting ride.

Are Manual Gearboxes a Relic of the Past?

By James May July 15, 2024

In an age of lightning-fast dual-clutch transmissions and seamless automatics, the humble manual gearbox seems increasingly like an anachronism. Is it time to finally bid farewell to the stick shift, or does it still hold a special place in the hearts of driving enthusiasts?

For many, the manual gearbox offers an unparalleled level of engagement, a true connection between driver and machine. The satisfaction of a perfectly rev-matched downshift, the precise control over the car's power delivery – these are experiences that automatics, however advanced, simply cannot replicate. However, for everyday driving in congested cities, the convenience of an automatic is undeniable. As electric vehicles become more prevalent, the very concept of a gearbox might become obsolete. We ponder the future of this beloved mechanical component.
